The Curtin Environmental Analytics Network is a group of more than 40 academics across three Schools at Curtin University. We are united in our passion for understanding the environment through analysis, but we each bring a different approach. Because of this, the network encompasses an astonishing breadth of expertise. There are not many problems in environmental analytics that we cannot solve.

CEAN is also dedicated to ongoing learning and training. We support a large postgraduate community, industry engagement, and ongoing learning through a range of dedicated activities including a coding club, a seminar series, and industry hackathons.
